Potato Leek Soup

Potato Leek Soup - Inspired by the soup Remy makes in Ratatouille, his soup is a perfect appetizer in smaller protons or a satisfying main on a night where soup feels like the best option. With minimal prep, the extra time it’ll spend on the stove gives you some time to do the dishes, get housework done, or just chill by the tv waiting for the soup to be done.



Prep: 20 mins

Cook: 50 mins

Total time: 70 mins
