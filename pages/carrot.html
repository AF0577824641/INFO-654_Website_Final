Carrot Cake

Carrot Cake - This Carrot Cake has stolen my heart, and as the best autumnal dessert (move over pumpkin pie!) it deserves to be celebrated. Be sure to make this for any event you go to, whether that be a birthday, Thanksgiving, or Friendsgiving, and you will surely be praised before the night is over.

Prep: 15 mins

Cook: 25 mins

Total time: 40 mins
