Mashed Potatoes

Caramelized Onion Mashed Potato - This unbelievable vegan recipe is sure to add some extra flavor to everyone's favorite side. Make sure you save time to caramelize the onions, as that is the longest time commitment in this recipe, but so worth it when those perfectly caramelised onions hit your mouth with buttery smooth potatoes as its bedding.
Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 40 mins
Total time: 55 mins
