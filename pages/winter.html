Winter

In the darkest coldest months, we are bringing you recipes that will brighten your days and warm your hearts. Starting out with a hearty lasagna soup, we are focusing on protein and carbs to get you feeling full and content. Who doesn’t love a good lasagna without more than half the work? Next is a potato and leek soup (inspired by Ratatouille, one of my favorite family movies) which introduces lots of vegetables and starches to your diet. Lastly on a dark night what better way to warm up than with a French inspired hot chocolate? Enjoy with a box of shortbread cookies and you’ll be feeling like a little kid on Christmas morning.
