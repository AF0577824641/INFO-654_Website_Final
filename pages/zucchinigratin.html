Zucchini Gratin

Zucchini Gratin - This gratin is great for dinner parties and doled out as a side for guests to enjoy all of its cheesy goodness. Make it on a day where you can stomach something a little bit heavier, as it requires an empty stomach to finish off this giant vegetable dish.

Prep: 15 mins

Cook: 45 mins

Total time: 60 mins
