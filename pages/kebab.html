Hawaiian Chicken Kabobs

Hawaiian Chicken Kabobs - Prepare yourself to taste chicken like you’ve never tasted before. This Hawaiian inspired recipe calls for chicken and pineapple cubes to make some of the most amazing skewers you’ve ever seen. Just sauce them up, throw them on the grill, and serve to your hungry family for a luau!

Prep: 25 mins

Cook: 15 mins

Total time: 40 mins
