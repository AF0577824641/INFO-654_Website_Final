Autumn

As the year winds down, we have to keep in mind the most American holiday there is: Thanksgiving! We have a few recipes that will wow your guests, starting off with a carrot cake that will become a staple after you’ve made it once. Everyone’s favorite sides have to be included, so we are starting out with a mac and cheese guaranteed to provide seconds (or thirds) to anyone who tries it. And last is vegan caramelized onion mashed potatoes which add a depth of flavor that other mashed potatoes simply do not have. Be sure to toast to the best food you’ve ever made and enjoy the holiday!
