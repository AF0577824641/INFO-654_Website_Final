Strawberry muffins

Strawberry Muffins - What says Spring more than strawberries? Nothing! Use your freshest batch of locally sourced strawberries to make these delectable muffins for an easy breakfast or a nighttime sweet treat.

1. Preheat oven to 375F. Line a muffin tin with paper liners.

2. In a large bowl whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, poppyseeds, lemon zest, and salt.

3. In another small bowl whisk eggs, honey, yogurt, oil, vanilla and almond extract until smooth. 4. Pour wet into dry and mix until just combined. Don't over mix!

5. Fold in strawberries.

6. Fill muffin cups up about ⅔ of the way.

7. Generously sprinkle with demerara.

8. Bake for 20-25 minutes until a toothpick inserted into the muffins comes out clean (a few moist crumbs are ok!)

9. Let cool in the pan for 5 minutes.

10. Transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. Enjoy! I love them slightly warm with salted butter too!

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 30 mins Total time: 40 mins
