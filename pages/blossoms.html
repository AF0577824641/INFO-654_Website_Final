Fried Squash Blossoms

Fried Squash Blossoms - As an homage to my Nonna, I’ve included my favorite summertime dish on this list to share the joy and flavors of it with you guys. Squash blossoms can be found at any farmer’s market at the end of summer so scoop them up, fill it with your cheese mixture, and fit the frying oil for a treat that takes minutes to prepare.

Prep: 25 mins

Cook: 5 mins

Total time: 30 mins
