Lasagna Soup

Lasagna Soup - Love a lasagna but hate the amount of work it takes to put together? Making it into a soup takes it from an hours long endeavor to a half hour of easy cooking. Try this on one of the coldest winter nights and watch as your entire home becomes a Tuscan vacation home.

Prep: 10 mins

Cook: 30 mins

Total time: 20 mins
